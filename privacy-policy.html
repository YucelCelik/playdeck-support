Privacy Policy

PlayDeck — Last updated: March 22, 2026

Introduction

PlayDeck ("we", "our", or "the app") is a game discovery application that lets you browse, save, and organize video games. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. PlayDeck does not require account creation, login, or registration. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

Locally Stored Data

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your saved games list (games you swiped right on)
  • Your favorites list
  • App preferences (language, theme)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the app.

Third-Party Services

PlayDeck uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — We display ads to support the app. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • RAWG Video Games Database — Game metadata (titles, images, ratings, descriptions) is sourced from RAWG. No personal data is shared with RAWG.

Children's Privacy

PlayDeck is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, your information remains securely on your device. We have no servers that store user data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
