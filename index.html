FAQ

How does PlayDeck work?

Browse games as cards. Swipe right to save a game to your list, swipe left to skip. It's that simple.

Is PlayDeck free?

Yes, PlayDeck is free to download and use. The app contains ads.

Where does the game data come from?

Game data is powered by the RAWG Video Games Database.

How do I remove a game from my list?

Go to My List or Favorites and tap the remove button on any game.
